About Shringar Groups

Shringar Group is a company that has great expertise as costumes designers and suppliers of Costumes for various Drama and Dance activities for all School and College programs.

We are situated in Bangalore which is the capital of Karnataka state, famous for its culture and tradition. We are in this field for more than 17 years and are considered to be a brand people can trust.

Shringar Group operates in multiple areas of culture and one of them is Costumes. We sell and also give on rent costumes of all kinds for both kids and adults. It includes but is not limited to Fancy Dress Costumes, Bharatnatyam Dance Costumes, Retro Costumes, Radha Krishna Costume and many more. The other prominent area in which we operate is jewellery. We deal in jewellery of all kinds like earrings, necklaces, bangles and more.

We do have jewellery products of both kinds - traditional and contemporary. Apart from jewellery there are a range of other products that you can find - idols, cosmetics, decoration rangolis, dance accessories, hair accessories and many more.
